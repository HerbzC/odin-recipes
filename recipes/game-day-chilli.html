Game Day Chilli

Description

This chilli is my favourite variation i've made - it's a good way to use whatever crap leftover beers people leave at my house

Can be served with rice - i prefer to have over jacket potatoes

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 500g beef mince
  • 2 small red onions, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 4 garlic cloves, grated
  • 1 tbsp sliced jalapenos
  • 1 tin tomato paste
  • 1 tin tomatoes
  • 1 bottle beer
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • (optional) drizzle of honey

Spices

  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tsp coriander seeds
