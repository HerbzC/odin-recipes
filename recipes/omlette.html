Game Day Chilli

Description

Easy recipe for a nice quick and delicious breakfast

I use 1 slice of cheese per egg, so scale accordingly

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 slices cheese

Method

  1. Heat pan on max
  2. Add oil - heat until almost smoking and spread round pan
  3. Add eggs - make sure to cover entire pan
  4. Place cheese slices on one half of omlette
  5. Once set, fold omlette in half over cheese
  6. Fold one final time into quarter
  7. Serve - I normally have on a flatbread with salad
